## Get Up and Running in 3 Minutes

### Step 1: Open the App (30 seconds)
1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. You'll see the Dashboard with empty stats
3. That's it - the app is ready to use!

### Step 2: Load Your Contacts (1 minute)
**Option A - Use Data Loader (Recommended)**
1. Open `data-loader.html` in your browser
2. Click "Choose your CSV file"
3. Select either:
   - `AdsellAI_Outreach_Tracker_Populated.csv` OR
   - `albany_ski_expo_vendor_contacts_UPDATED.csv`
4. Click "Load Data into App"
5. Click "Open Outreach Tracker"

**Option B - Manual Import**
1. In the app, click "Import CSV" in the navigation
2. Upload your CSV file
3. Preview and confirm import

### Step 3: Start Tracking (1-2 minutes)
1. Go to "Contacts" page
2. Click on any contact to view details
3. Click "Log Activity" to record your first outreach
4. Try using the "Scripts" page for email templates

## Common Questions

**Q: Where is my data stored?**  
A: In your browser's localStorage. It's safe and private, but only on this computer/browser.

**Q: Can my team access the same data?**  
A: Not automatically. Either use separate tracking or export/import CSV files to share.

**Q: What if I lose my data?**  
A: No automatic backups. Export to CSV regularly or use the browser's export function.

**Q: Can I access this on mobile?**  
A: Yes! The interface is fully responsive and works on phones/tablets.

**Q: Do I need internet?**  
A: No! The app works completely offline once loaded.
